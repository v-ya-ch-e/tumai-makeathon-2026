# Challenge Overview — Reply @ TUM.ai Makeathon 2026

> One-page orientation sourced from the Notion [Participant Page Makeathon 2026](https://www.notion.so/ff5ce27ee4b28229b7888147af6a71d2). For the full challenge text and tooling, see the GitHub-derived files linked below.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor:** Reply (Data Reply).
- **Challenge title:** *The Campus Co-Pilot Suite — Agents for Students by Students.*
- **Goal:** Build an autonomous AI agent (or multi-agent system) that takes concrete actions across TUM's fragmented digital ecosystem (TUMonline, Moodle, Library, ZHS, Mensa, Matrix, Confluence, etc.) and fundamentally improves student life.
- **Venue room:** **1100** (`0501.01.100`) — used for Reply Q&A sessions and final pitches. [nav.tum.de link](https://nav.tum.de/en/room/0501.01.100).
- **Communication:** Notion for info, [Discord](https://discord.gg/RejrDuQ4m) for real-time chat with organizers and challenge setters.
- **Contact fallback:** `makeathon@tum-ai.com`.

## What evaluators score (from Reply)

1. **Innovation & Ambition** — creative agent architecture, real logistical/educational problems.
2. **Integration Depth & Autonomy** — cleanly interacts with real platforms and *acts*, not just retrieves.
3. **Real-World Impact & Overall Quality** — genuinely alleviates student friction.
4. **User Interface & User Experience** — intuitive, frictionless interaction.
5. **Presentation Quality** — pain point, solution, and impact all land in the final pitch.
